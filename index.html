🌹 Eggless Rose Milk Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Maida (All-purpose flour)
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 1 tsp Baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/4 cup Oil
  • 1 tsp Vanilla essence
  • 1 tbsp Rose syrup
  • Few drops pink food color

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and baking soda.
  3. In another bowl mix sugar, milk, oil, vanilla, rose syrup and color.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Pour batter into greased tray.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes.
  7. Prepare rose milk and pour over cake.
  8. Add whipped cream and pistachios.
