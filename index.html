🥛 Masala Milk Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Milk
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 4 Almonds (Badam)
  • 4 Pistachios
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom powder
  • A pinch of Saffron
  • A pinch of Turmeric (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil the milk in a pan.
  2. Add sugar and stir well.
  3. Add crushed almonds and pistachios.
  4. Add cardamom powder and saffron.
  5. Simmer for 3–5 minutes.
  6. Pour into a glass and serve hot or chilled.
