Maravalli Kilangu Parotta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups boiled Maravalli Kilangu (Tapioca)
  • 1 cup wheat flour
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • Oil for cooking

Preparation Steps

  1. Peel and boil the maravalli kilangu until soft.
  2. Mash the boiled tapioca well.
  3. Add wheat flour, onion, green chili, ginger, turmeric, cumin, and salt.
  4. Mix everything and knead into a soft dough.
  5. Make small balls and roll into round parottas.
  6. Heat a tawa and cook the parotta with oil.
  7. Cook both sides until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot with curry or chutney.
