Mulappicha Muthira Thoran

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sprouted horse gram (Muthira)
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut
  • 2 dried red chillies
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp coconut oil

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat coconut oil in a pan.
  2. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add red chillies, curry leaves, and chopped onion.
  4. Sauté until onion becomes soft.
  5. Add sprouted horse gram and turmeric powder.
  6. Cook for 5–7 minutes.
  7. Add grated coconut and mix well.
  8. Cook for 2 minutes and serve hot.
